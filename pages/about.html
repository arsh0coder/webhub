About WebHub

WebHub is a community-driven platform offering a wide collection of free, open-source HTML, CSS, and JavaScript website templates. Our mission is to empower developers and designers worldwide by providing beautifully crafted templates ready to be used and customized.

Our Mission

To foster collaboration by providing a free, accessible repository of website templates. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned pro, WebHub helps you find clean, modern templates to kickstart your projects or inspire your creativity.

How to Contribute

  1. Fork our GitHub repository and clone it locally.
  2. Create a new branch for your template or improvements.
  3. Add your template files or make modifications.
  4. Test thoroughly for responsiveness and accessibility.
  5. Submit a pull request with clear description and demo links.
  6. Engage with our review process for feedback and approval.
